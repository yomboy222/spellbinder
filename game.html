Spell-Binder Prototype 2
While hiking in the Black Forest of Germany, you stumble upon a Medieval tower. Inside are stone runes, and old tomes ... bearing the name-plate of the fabled alchemist, Gombrecht the Irrefrugnable! Gombrecht had researched spells for transmutation, hoping to turn lead into gold. His idea was that by altering the letters in an object's True Name, you alter the object itself, for instance turning a Tier (beast) into a Bier (beer). His fatal mistake — obvious to us Americans — is that an object's True Name is the word for it in English, not German.

Like any self-respecting German alchemist, Gombrecht was highly organized, and kept his spells on separate pages in a Binder, which you find just inside the tower. As you explore the tower you may find more pages!
